About

GraphAR is an augmented reality visualization solution for graphs. Our mission is to redefine how people see and interact with mathematical concepts. In today’s classrooms, these complex graphs are drawn on whiteboards and printed on textbooks; however, two-dimensional mediums compromise the student’s grasp of the material, especially as the student progresses to higher level math courses. With GraphAR, people can better understand and interact with graphs intuitively in 3D space.
